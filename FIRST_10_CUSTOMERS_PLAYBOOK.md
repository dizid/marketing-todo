# First 10 Customers Playbook

## Overview

A focused 7-task journey for indie SaaS founders who need customers NOW.

**Target**: Indie dev launching SaaS
**Goal**: First 10 paying customers
**Timeline**: 7 days

---

## The 7 Steps

| Step | Task ID | Name | Purpose |
|------|---------|------|---------|
| 1 | setup-1 | Define Target Audience | Know exactly who you're selling to |
| 2 | growth-5 | Positioning & Messaging Map | Why should they choose you? |
| 3 | sales-2 | High-Converting Offer Builder | Create an irresistible launch offer |
| 4 | setup-2 | Set Up Landing Page | Where they sign up |
| 5 | growth-2 | Cold Outreach Campaigns | Direct outreach templates |
| 6 | acq-1 | Post in Communities | Find and engage your tribe |
| 7 | sales-3 | Objection Handling | Close the sale |
